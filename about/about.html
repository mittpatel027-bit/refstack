About Refstack

Refstack is a website with the capabillities of searching through a database of reference images. These images are created or uploaded by artists for other artists. Reference images can vary from actual paintings to simple images of real objects or anatomy. Refstack was created in response to the overwhelming amount of misinformation spread online due to computer generated images.
